About Us

At Sentimeter, we believe in the power of emotions to transform the learning experience. Our cutting-edge platform is designed to enhance engagement, foster connections, and provide invaluable feedback. Our mission is to empower educators and students both with emotional insights to create a more engaging and effective environment.



How it works

Sentimeter utilizes advanced emotion recognition technology to analyze and interpret students' emotional responses during online classes.
Through the seamless integration of AI and real-time data, we empower tutors to:

  • Gain Deeper Insights: Understand the emotional dynamics of your virtual classroom.
  • Enhance Engagement: Tailor your teaching approach to keep students actively involved.
  • Receive Immediate Feedback: Access real-time emotional feedback for quick adjustments.
  • Improve Learning Outcomes: Create a positive and effective learning environment.



Why Sentimeter

Our mission is to bridge the emotional gap in online education, helping tutors and students connect on a deeper level.
With Sentimeter, you can:
  • Save Time: No need for manual feedback collection; Sentimeter does it for you.
  • Boost Student Success: Adapt your teaching to address emotional needs.
  • Increase Satisfaction: Happy, engaged students lead to satisfied tutors.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Make informed decisions based on comprehensive emotional data.
